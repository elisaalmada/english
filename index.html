How Classes Work üí¨

My classes are dynamic and designed especially for you. Here's how we usually conduct our sessions:

  • What you will learn: Our classes are tailor-made! We focus on your fluency, grammar, vocabulary, and comprehension, always connecting English with your daily life and/or work reality. The teaching methodology aims to connect language, culture, knowledge, and society, promoting learning that empowers you to use English autonomously and meaningfully.
  • Duration and Frequency: Each class lasts 60 minutes. We'll agree on the best days and times for classes at the beginning of each month, and I recommend a minimum of 1 class per week for consistent progress.
  • Scheduling and Cancellation:
    • By the Student: Please notify at least 24 hours in advance for rescheduling. Rescheduling requests with less than 24 hours' notice are not feasible and will not be accepted. Rescheduled classes that cannot be accommodated within the same month, even with prior notice, will be considered as held and will not be refunded or transferred to the following month. Absences without notice will be counted as a held class and charged normally, with no right to make up the class.
    • By the Teacher: If I cannot teach the class, I will notify you as soon as possible. We will reschedule at no cost to you, or I will refund you for the unheld class.
  • Class Materials: The materials (slides, texts, video links, etc.) are provided by me and are included in the class price, with no extra costs. We use Google Classroom, Docs, Slides, Meet, and YouTube to make classes dynamic and interesting.
  • Confidentiality: All discussions and information shared during classes will be kept strictly confidential.
  • Start and End: This agreement begins with our first class and has no set end date. If either you or the teacher needs to stop classes for any reason, simply provide notice before the start of the next month. If classes are interrupted by the student, they will not receive a refund for classes scheduled within the current month.
  • Official Communication: We will use WhatsApp as the official form of communication. (+55 37 99125-4295)

I'm here to accompany you on this journey, making every step in your learning an enriching and liberating experience!

Proficiency Level Test üìù

Take this test to get an idea of your English proficiency level. It covers grammar and vocabulary. At the end, you'll be able to see your results and copy them to share with me!

1. He usually ______ (go) to work by car, but today he ______ (walk).

2. While I ______ (read) a book, my phone ______ (ring).

3. I ______ (not see) her since last year.

4. I think it ______ (rain) tomorrow. (Prediction)

5. You ______ study harder if you want to pass the exam. (Strong advice/necessity)

6. If it ______ (rain) tomorrow, we ______ (stay) home.

7. If I ______ (have) a million dollars, I ______ (buy) a big house.

8. The book ______ (write) by a famous author last year.

9. She has ______ cat and ______ dog. ______ cat is black.

10. The meeting is ______ 3 PM ______ Friday.

11. The book is ______ the table, ______ the lamp.

12. The plane will ______ in ten minutes. (Meaning: take off from the ground)

13. If you don't know the word, you can ______ it ______ in a dictionary. (Meaning: find its meaning)

14. She is ______ (tall) than her brother. He runs ______ (quickly) than me. (Comparison)

15. There isn't ______ water. There are ______ books.

16. By the time I arrived, he ______ (already leave).

17. She said, "I am happy." (Reported Speech)

18. If you heat water to 100 degrees Celsius, it ______ (boil).

19. This is the student ______ helped me with my homework.

20. By next year, I ______ (learn) English for five years.

Writing Part: Write a short paragraph (3-5 sentences) about your favorite hobby or a recent travel experience.
