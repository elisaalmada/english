Understanding 'Little' & 'A Little'

We use these with uncountable nouns (e.g., water, time, money). `Little` means 'almost none' (negative feeling), while `A little` means 'some' (positive feeling).

Understanding 'Few' & 'A Few'

We use these with countable nouns (e.g., cities, days, euros). `Few` means 'almost none' (negative feeling), while `A few` means 'some' (positive feeling).
